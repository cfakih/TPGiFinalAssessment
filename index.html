Strategic Implementation Assessment

TPGi Sales Operations Evaluation

Executive Summary

style="margin-bottom: 20px;"TPGi's planned migration from HubSpot to Salesforce creates a critical opportunity to enhance sales operations beyond current capabilities. While extending HubSpot contracts provides short-term continuity, the evaluation revealed significant operational constraints limiting growth potential across sequence management, task execution, coaching visibility, and conversation intelligence.

style="margin-bottom: 20px;"The recommended Outreach implementation would consolidate multiple point solutions into a unified platform, delivering enterprise-grade automation, advanced reporting capabilities, and conversation intelligence that transforms how the organization manages its 20-30 concurrent sales initiatives and supports its accessibility-focused mission.

style="margin-bottom: 20px;"Investment Framework: Platform licensing scales from $120-$230 per user monthly, with 3-4 week implementation including custom training, workflow consulting, and Salesforce integration (backed by Salesforce's strategic investment in Outreach).

Risk Mitigation: Outreach's enterprise-grade Salesforce integration and accessibility considerations provide a more scalable foundation than current HubSpot limitations, supporting TPGi's unique requirements serving the low-vision community.

Process Workflow Analysis

Sequence Management & Content Optimization

Current Process: HubSpot forces choice between A/B testing OR dynamic sequences, limiting optimization capabilities. Content marketer cannot test messaging variations while maintaining engagement-based automation.

Impact: Reduced conversion rates, suboptimal messaging, limited data-driven decision making across 20-30 concurrent initiatives.

Resource Cost: Marketing team creates content without performance feedback, SDR team executes sequences without optimization insights.

Daily Task Execution & Prioritization

Current Process: SDRs manually filter and prioritize 200-400 daily tasks across multiple sequences and campaigns. Limited automation for task scheduling and routing.

Impact: Productivity bottlenecks during high-volume periods, inconsistent follow-up timing, manual rescheduling of overdue tasks.

Resource Cost: 30-45 minutes daily per SDR on task management instead of prospect engagement.

CRM Integration & Workflow Automation

Current Process: HubSpot provides basic automation with limited Salesforce connectivity. Complex triggers require manual configuration and monitoring.

Impact: Reduced automation capabilities, manual data management, limited trigger sophistication for complex sales motions.

Resource Cost: Operations team manages workarounds instead of focusing on strategic initiatives.

Coaching & Performance Management

Current Process: Fireflies records calls but systematic coaching processes not implemented. Limited visibility into rep performance drivers and messaging effectiveness.

Impact: Inconsistent messaging, missed coaching opportunities, difficulty scaling best practices across team.

Resource Cost: Management time spent on reactive coaching instead of proactive performance optimization.

Conversation Intelligence & Deal Insights

Current Process: Separate Gong evaluation creates tool sprawl. Call insights disconnected from sequence performance and deal progression data.

Impact: Fragmented insights, additional vendor management, disconnected performance data across sales activities.

Resource Cost: Leadership evaluating multiple solutions instead of unified platform approach.

Advanced Reporting & Analytics

Current Process: Limited reporting granularity in HubSpot. Cannot differentiate positive vs negative email replies or compare sequence performance effectively.

Impact: Reduced visibility into campaign effectiveness, limited data-driven optimization opportunities.

Resource Cost: Manual reporting and analysis instead of automated insights and recommendations.
